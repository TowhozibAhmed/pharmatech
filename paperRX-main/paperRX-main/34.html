We'll cut to the chase. Are you…

  • A mid- to senior-level UX designer? (5+ years experience)
  • Passionate about solving user and business problems with Design Thinking?
  • A wiz with beautiful and organized Figma components and design systems?
  • Unafraid to tackle complex problems in complex systems?
  • Great at communicating your ideas to clients and developers?

Then join our team!

RTS Labs is a solid, fast-growing software development agency specializing in enterprise web and mobile applications. We work with a wide range of clients — from startups to Fortune 500 companies, across many different industries.

We're looking for a mid- to senior-level Product Designer. As a member of the Product team, you'll be the voice of the user and the product ambassador. This includes:

Creating meaningful and innovative digital experiences for users, and balancing these needs with business goals and constraints Serving as a Product leader and liaison, both externally (with clients) and internally (with the engineering team)

What we offer:

  • A competitive salary based on experience
  • 401(k) with a 4% company match
  • Family-first environment with paid parental leave and flexible PTO
  • Gym membership discounts
  • Health, vision, life, and short-term disability insurance
  • 40 hours a year for professional development to use how you choose
  • A culture of enthusiasm and personal growth, where you'll have the power to shape the direction of RTS Labs, with no micro-management
  • Remote/hybrid work with flexible hours

What you'll do as Senior Product Designer:

  • Build empathy and be a user advocate
  • Conduct user and stakeholder interviews, usability tests, and Design Thinking workshops
  • Collaborate with clients and BSAs to inform business and functional requirements
  • Produce visual artifacts to inform feature prioritization, including user flows, information architecture diagrams, and wireframes
  • Conduct content audits and plan information architecture
  • Produce pixel-perfect high-fidelity designs and prototypes using consistent UI components that meet brand standards
  • Leverage analytics tools to measure success, present opportunities, and support decision-making
  • Liaise with clients and developers to support product planning and implementation

What you need to succeed:

  • 5+ years experience in UX design, preferably in software product development
  • Experience with Design Thinking process and workshop facilitation
  • Expert skills with mobile-first responsive web design
  • Experience using design systems or component libraries
  • Experience with Figma for high-fidelity designs and robust prototyping (We use Figma but experience with similar tools — Adobe XD, Sketch, etc — is okay too!)
  • Ability to clearly and concisely defend design decisions and explain priorities to clients and internal teams
  • Be a leader and mentor: You'll challenge, grow and contribute to fostering a collaborative team spirit

Bonus points for:

  • Experience with complex web applications, digital engagement and/or SaaS products
  • Ability to understand complex systems by viewing them through the lens of OOUX (object-oriented UX)
  • Experience with native mobile application design (iOS and Android)
  • Experience with Lean and Agile methodologies
  • Knowledge of accessibility, psychology, usability heuristics
  • Familiarity with popular design systems (Apple's Human Interface guidelines, Google's Material Design, and Chakra UI)
  • Familiarity with basics of HTML, CSS, Javascript
